GardenIQ — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

GardenIQ ("we", "us", or "our") is developed and operated by BarksIQ. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the GardenIQ mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Your email address when you register an account
  • Garden data: Plants you add to your garden, watering dates, and personal notes
  • Location data: Approximate location (only with your permission) to provide climate-based garden advice
  • Usage data: Search history within the app to improve your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalised garden advice, plant care information, and a garden calendar
  • To generate AI-powered recommendations based on your garden and location
  • To process and manage your Pro subscription
  • To improve and develop the app

3. Third-Party Services

GardenIQ uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — secure database and user authentication
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered garden and plant advice
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management
  • PlantNet — plant identification via photo
  • Trefle — plant database for search results
  • Open-Meteo — weather data for watering advice
  • iNaturalist — pest and disease images

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted in the European Union. We take appropriate technical measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

GardenIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
