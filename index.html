GardenIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

GardenIQ ("we", "us", or "the app") respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data the GardenIQ app collects, why, and how it is used and retained.

1. What data we collect

  • Account information - when you sign in with Google (OAuth), we receive your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Google, to create and manage your GardenIQ account.
  • Photos - if you use the plant scanning feature, we process photos you take with your camera or select from your gallery, in order to identify plants. Photos are used for plant identification and are not shared with third parties for other purposes.
  • Location data - with your permission, we use your approximate location or the country you provide to show the correct climate zone advice and weather information.
  • Garden and plant data - plants you add to your garden, lawn data, and calendar notes you enter yourself.
  • Purchase and subscription data - if you subscribe to GardenIQ Pro, our payment processor (RevenueCat, via Google Play/the App Store) handles the transaction. We only receive your subscription status, not payment details such as card numbers.
  • Usage data - technical information such as device type and app version, used for debugging and improving the app.

2. Third-party services

GardenIQ relies on the following external services to function:

  • Supabase - account management and data storage
  • Google Sign-In - app login
  • PlantNet and Trefle - plant identification and plant information
  • Anthropic (Claude API) - AI-generated care advice
  • Open-Meteo - weather information
  • RevenueCat - managing Pro subscriptions
  • Expo/EAS - app updates and push notifications

These providers may process the data required to deliver their service, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Why we use this data

We use your data only to:

  • manage your account and let you sign in;
  • identify plants and provide tailored care advice;
  • maintain your garden calendar and reminders;
  • activate your Pro subscription correctly;
  • improve the app and fix technical issues.

We never sell your data to third parties, and we do not show third-party ads based on your data.

4. Data retention and account deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you have a GardenIQ account.

To request deletion of your account and associated data: email [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS HERE] from the address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete account". Please include the email address of your GardenIQ account.

After a deletion request, within 30 days we remove: your account details (name, email), your garden and plant data, uploaded photos, and your subscription link. Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics that cannot be traced back to you may be retained for product improvement.

5. Children

GardenIQ is not specifically directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental or guardian consent.
